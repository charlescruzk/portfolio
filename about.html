About Me

Hello, my name is Charles Cruz

I'm a photo retoucher with more than a decade of experience making pixels behave. From fashion lookbooks to high-volume e-commerce shoots, I've color corrected, masked, and polished it all.

When I'm not elbow deep in layers, I'm probably cracking dad jokes or chasing the perfect cup of coffee—sometimes both at once.

Fun fact: during my time in the U.S. Navy I had a near-death experience. Apparently Heaven had a waiting list, because God told me there were photos that still needed retouching.
